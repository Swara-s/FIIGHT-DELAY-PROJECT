About This Project

This final-year major project predicts flight delay probability and estimated delay minutes by combining historical records with operational indicators such as airline, airport, departure/arrival slots, and weather.

Project Highlights
  • Flask-based full-stack web platform
  • SQLite persistence for users and prediction history
  • ML pipeline with preprocessing, training, evaluation, and model persistence
  • Interactive analytics dashboard powered by Chart.js
